<h1><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mycroft AI Kickstarter History</span></h1>
[/vc_column_text][divider line_type="No Line" custom_height="30"][vc_column_text]
<h4><span style="font-weight: 400;">History</span></h4>
<span style="font-weight: 400;">In the fall of 2015 we went to Kickstarter to see if there was a market for our idea. We raised $193,000 USD and turned Mycroft from a project into a company.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">We fulfilled all crowdfunding orders in July of 2017 - putting Mark I units in the hands of 1500 backers.</span>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">Between these two dates we’ve progressed rapidly on the software and business development front, as detailed in the next section </span><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">Company History and Traction.</span></i>

<span style="font-weight: 400;">Our first Kickstarter campaign sold an advance prototype, Mark I. Still an open source, open hardware voice assistant, it was targeted at the makers and hackers. Mark II is for </span><i><span style="font-weight: 400;">all </span></i><span style="font-weight: 400;">consumers. A more robust voice assistant with far more benefits, and an increasingly intelligent technology stack behind it.</span>
<h4><span style="font-weight: 400;">About this campaign</span></h4>
<span style="font-weight: 400;">With all the lessons we learned from the production of Mark I, and feedback from our community and customers, it was time for an upgrade. Thus, the second kickstarter--and a way for us to flip the retail consumer hardware market on its head.</span>
<h4><span style="font-weight: 400;">Mycroft is working to raise $300,000 on Kickstarter by the end of the campaign. </span></h4>
<span style="font-weight: 400;">The backers' support will help us achieve our goal of bringing voice AI to everyone without the constraints that the others place on you!
