<h5>Voice is the future.</h5>
How will your customers interact with you in the future? Using voice. How will your customers interact with service agents? Using voice. How will your potential customers find you? Using voice. But the key companies in this space - Google, Amazon, Microsoft and Apple - see voice are building voice on their terms.
<h5>Mycroft is different.</h5>
Mycroft offers a brandable, open source voice stack that can be integrated into your enterprise offering - automotive, elder care or industrial settings. Mycroft is data private and independent from incumbents.
